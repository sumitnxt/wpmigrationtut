If you have started wordpress development for your site then right at the beginning its important to know that , " do not touch the customizing url settings" . it may trouble you if you do , during wordpress migration. Once you have finished the site. the last thing can be tweak the url settings. And even then realize that you may have to migrate the site in future. If you have already tweaked url settings. Your best effort would be to set it back to default , although it may still give trouble.
